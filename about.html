Hari Bakery

Hari Bakery is a seasonal cafe and marketplace featuring fresh baked goods in Ranipettai D.T, run by Pastry Chef/Baker Prasanth and Dhiva. Longtime champions of produce-driven cooking, Prasanth and Dhiva built their careers over fifteen years in renowned kitchens of Ranipet(including, Lucques, Csmpsnile, and Cooks County) before opening Hari Bakery.

At Hari Bakery, they continue their dedication to hand made food with artisan breads and pastries made daily with locally-sourced, heirloom grains, as well as a produce forward day-time menu.
